## 📋 INSTAGRAM POSTING STRATEGY

### Post Format Recommendations:

**Option A: Carousel (10 slides in one post)**
- Best for: Telling the full story
- Engagement: High (people swipe through)
- Hashtags: 25-30 strategic + niche
- Caption: Hook in first line, CTA at end

**Option B: Story Series (1 slide = 1 story)**
- Best for: Building anticipation
- Engagement: Sequential engagement
- Duration: Post 1/day for 10 days
- Mention: "Swipe to next story" + CTA

**Option C: Reels (animate slides)**
- Best for: Maximum viral reach
- Music: Upbeat, professional, trending
- Duration: 15-30 seconds total
- Text overlays: Key metrics pop-up style

### Key Hashtags:
`#PerisAI #DataIntelligence #Analytics #AI #Indonesia #Fintech #SaaS #DataScience #BondMarkets #Finance #TreasuryManagement #FundManagement #NoCode #EmergingMarkets #IndonesiaTech`

### Best Posting Times (Indonesia):
- Weekdays: 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 6-7 PM
- Engagement tends highest: Tuesday-Thursday

### CTA Strategy:
- Slide 1-9: Build curiosity → "Swipe to next"
- Slide 10: Direct CTA → "DM us" or "Link in bio"
