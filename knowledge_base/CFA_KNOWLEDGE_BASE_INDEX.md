# CFA Knowledge Base Index
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-11

This document indexes the comprehensive CFA curriculum materials available for /kei and /kin persona training.

## Index Structure

### 1. Quantitative Methods (01_QuantitativeMethods)
**Modules:** 47 lessons covering statistical, probabilistic, and mathematical foundations
- **Time Value of Money:** Annualized returns, discounting, present value
- **Statistical Analysis:** Return measures, hypothesis testing, confidence intervals
- **Probability & Estimation:** Probability trees, conditional expectations, simulation
- **Regression Analysis:** Simple linear regression, parametric models, big data techniques
- **Portfolio Mathematics:** Portfolio variance, correlation, optimization
- **Key Application:** Bond yield forecasting, risk decomposition, return attribution

### 2. Economics (02_Economics)
**Modules:** 33 lessons on macroeconomic, monetary, and policy frameworks
- **Business Cycles:** Cyclical analysis, recession dynamics, growth phases
- **Monetary Policy:** Central bank operations (BI rate for Indonesia), inflation targeting
- **Fiscal Policy:** Government spending, taxation, debt sustainability
- **International Trade:** Trade flows, tariffs, competitive advantages
- **FX Markets:** Exchange rate determination, capital flows, currency dynamics
- **Geopolitics:** Trade relationships, sanctions, strategic considerations
- **Exchange Rate Calculations:** Spot rates, forward rates, PPP, IRP
- **Key Application:** Indonesian macro context (BI policy, IDR/USD dynamics, fiscal constraints)

### 3. Corporate Issuers (03_CorporateIssuers)
**Modules:** 20 lessons on corporate structure, governance, and strategy
- **Business Forms:** Organizational structures, ownership, public vs. private
- **Stakeholder Claims:** Equity vs. debt, priority rankings, governance
- **Working Capital:** Cash conversion cycle, liquidity management
- **Capital Allocation:** CapEx, dividends, share buybacks, M&A
- **Capital Structure:** Debt vs. equity, cost of capital, optimal leverage (Modigliani-Miller)
- **Business Models:** Revenue streams, competitive positioning, industry dynamics
- **Key Application:** Understanding issuer credit quality (relevant for sovereign debt comparison)

### 4. Financial Statement Analysis (04_FSA)
**Modules:** 40 lessons on accounting, statement analysis, and modeling
- **Framework:** IFRS vs. GAAP, source documents, reporting quality
- **Income Statement:** Revenue recognition, expense timing, EPS, quality analysis
- **Balance Sheet:** Assets, liabilities, equity claims, intangibles, revaluations
- **Cash Flow Statement:** Operating/investing/financing activities, quality of earnings
- **Financial Ratios:** Liquidity, solvency, profitability, activity, ROE decomposition
- **Model Building:** Forecasting, scenario analysis, inflation effects
- **Key Application:** Assessing government financial health (debt ratios, fiscal sustainability)

### 5. Equity Investment (05_EquityInvestment)
**Modules:** 37 lessons on markets, indexing, valuation, and analysis
- **Markets & Systems:** Market structure, settlement, regulation, efficiency
- **Indexes:** Construction, rebalancing, equity vs. fixed-income indexes
- **Market Efficiency:** EMH, anomalies, behavioral deviations
- **Equity Features:** Characteristics, dividends, growth/value, risk/return
- **Valuation Models:** DDM, FCFE, P/E, EV/EBITDA, asset-based models
- **Industry Analysis:** Structure, competitive positioning, forecasting
- **Key Application:** Comparative yield analysis vs. equity risk premium

### 6. Fixed Income (06_FixedIncome) — **PRIMARY FOR BOND ANALYSIS**
**Modules:** 72 lessons on bonds, credit, and structured products
- **Bond Fundamentals:** Features, indentures, covenants, legal framework
- **Bond Types:** Corporate, sovereign, quasi-government, asset-backed
- **Pricing & Yields:** Time value, yield measures, annualized returns, conventions
- **Yield Curves:** Spot rates, forward rates, par curves, term structure interpretation
- **Duration & Convexity:** Macaulay/modified duration, key rate duration, empirical duration
- **Bond Returns:** Carry, roll-down, duration, FX impacts, total return decomposition
- **Credit Risk:** Rating agencies, sovereign analysis, credit spreads
- **Securitization:** Structured products, ABS, RMBS, CMBS
- **Key Application:** Indonesian bond analysis framework
  - Readings 17-18: **Sovereign Debt (highly relevant)**
  - Readings 21-50: **Bond Pricing, Yields, and Risk Measures**
  - Readings 53-59: **Credit Risk and Sovereign Analysis**

### 7. Derivatives (07_Derivatives)
**Modules:** 51 lessons on forwards, futures, options, and swaps
- **Contracts:** Forwards, futures, swaps, options, credit derivatives
- **Markets:** OTC vs. exchange-traded, clearing, settlement
- **Pricing:** Forward contracts, interest rate forwards, futures, swaps
- **Options:** Valuation, Greeks, put-call parity, binomial models
- **Applications:** Hedging, speculation, arbitrage
- **Key Application:** Bond duration hedging, FX forward contracts for rupiah exposure

### 8. Alternative Investments (08_AlternativeInvestments)
**Modules:** 26 lessons on private capital, real assets, and hedge funds
- **Private Equity & Debt:** Characteristics, returns, diversification
- **Real Estate & Infrastructure:** Features, investment characteristics
- **Commodities:** Natural resources, commodity investing
- **Hedge Funds:** Strategies, performance, risk/return
- **Digital Assets:** Blockchain, cryptocurrencies, token assets
- **Key Application:** Diversification context, institutional investor behavior

### 9. Portfolio Management (09_PortfolioManagement)
**Modules:** 64 lessons on theory, construction, and risk management
- **Modern Portfolio Theory:** Efficient frontier, risk-return optimization
- **CAPM:** Beta, security market line, cost of equity
- **Asset Allocation:** Strategic asset allocation, tactical positioning
- **IPS Development:** Constraints, objectives, client profiling
- **Behavioral Finance:** Cognitive biases, emotional biases, market implications
- **Risk Management:** Framework, governance, risk budgeting, monitoring
- **Key Application:** Portfolio duration, bond allocation decisions, ESG considerations

### 10. Ethical Standards (10_EthicalProfessionalStandards)
**Modules:** 51 lessons on professional conduct and integrity
- **Code of Ethics:** CFA Institute standards, professionalism, independence
- **Market Integrity:** Insider trading, market manipulation
- **Client Duties:** Suitability, fair dealing, confidentiality
- **Conflicts of Interest:** Disclosure, priority of transactions
- **GIPS Standards:** Performance presentation standards
- **Key Application:** Transparency in analysis, avoiding conflicts, professional judgment

## Indonesian Bond Analysis Framework (from CFA Materials)

**Reading 17-18: Sovereign Debt**
- Government bond characteristics (maturity, coupon structure, indentures)
- Sovereign credit risk factors (fiscal sustainability, external balance, FX reserves)
- Bond issuance and trading mechanics
- Yield spread determinants and credit spreads

**Reading 21-27: Bond Pricing & Yields**
- Price-yield relationship and convexity
- Yield measures (YTM, YTW, running yield)
- Spot rates, forward rates, par curves
- Yield spread measures for comparative analysis

**Reading 32-50: Duration & Term Structure**
- Macaulay and modified duration for interest rate sensitivity
- Key rate duration for yield curve positioning
- Convexity effects on bond returns
- Spot, par, and forward yield curves

**Reading 36-45: Bond Return Decomposition**
- Sources of return: carry, roll-down, duration gain/loss, FX
- Investment horizon effects
- Macro factor analysis (rates, spreads, FX)

**Reading 53-59: Credit Risk & Analysis**
- Sovereign credit analysis methodology
- Rating agency frameworks
- Yield spread drivers
- Default probability and recovery rates
